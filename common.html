Anxiety disorders such as Generalised anxiety disorder, panic disorders (attacks) and Obsessive-compulsive disorder (OCD), depression, and eating disorders are increasingly prevalent in youth.

Anxiety Disorders

Anxiety disorders are the most common types of mental health problems.
The individual has a severe fear or anxiety, which is linked to certain objects or situations.
Most people with an anxiety disorder will try to avoid exposure to whatever triggers their anxiety.

Some examples include:

Generalised anxiety disorder
Panic disorder/attack
Obsessive-compulsive disorder (OCD)
